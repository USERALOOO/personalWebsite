Zurich is a really beautiful city which is located in Switzerland. It is known for its nature and silence. It is very famous for its beauty also. People

People of switzerland are very known for their high living standard, punctuality, and efficiency. It is a multidiverse place with people of different culture their. People who live in Zurich are considered to be finacially good.

Culture

They usually speak Swiss-German as their prevalent language, however, English is commonly used everywhere over Zurich too.

Food

Cheese and Chocolate are very well known in the Swiss cuisine where it can be found in tradition Swiss people or modern high quality restaurant.
